D3 Forum

This module allows you to manage forums but also comments of any D3 module.

D3 Forum is a duplicatable module used to create and manage forums and forum entries.

XCL D3 forum module has been around for a long time.

It was developed by Gijoe and Jidaikobo, improved by Naao, Nao-pon, Domifara.

The Forum module has many of the same features as VBulletin and phpBB, but it's also tightly integrated into XCL.

Categories and Forums

Once activated in the Settings > Module Management

you must first create a Category and a forum from the front-end.

Then you will be redirected to get access to the module advanced options.

Permissions

Provide permissions to groups and users.

Category and Forum permissions are a combination of a category, forum, forum users, and permissions. Associate forum users and permissions within a specific category and forum.

Forum Blocks

Adding A Forum Block

Another nice feature you can add blocks with posts, topics and forums to your site provided by the module. The module creates a block that you can enable to show on the pages of modules you select.

Preferences

You'll now see the general settings area.

These settings focus primarily on how the forums, forum topics, and comments are set on the site. The D3 Forum module comes prepackage with several options for your forum.

Review the list of options and define your preferences.

If you are familiar with themes and templates, you can make a copy (clone/duplicate) of the module templates files to customize. Then, you can edit the files and CSS to meet your theme needs.
